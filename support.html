FAQ (Quick)

How do I get help with a purchase?

Send your Apple receipt and a short description to support@rowxl.com.

Where is my data stored?

We use Supabase (EU region) for secure authentication and storage. See Privacy for details.

How do I delete my account/data?

Email support@rowxl.com from the address used in-app. We’ll confirm and process deletion within 7 days.

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what we collect and why. It applies to the RowXL iOS app and this website.

What we collect

  • Account info (email) for sign-in and support.
  • Uploaded training media and analysis reports, stored securely and auto-deleted per in-app policy.
  • Basic diagnostics (crash logs, performance) to improve stability.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve RowXL features.
  • Offer support when you contact us.
  • Comply with legal requirements and App Store policies.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. We use processors (e.g., Apple, Supabase) under strict agreements.

Security

Transport is encrypted via HTTPS. Access is restricted and logged. You can request deletion at any time.
